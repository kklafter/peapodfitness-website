  • First Trimester Focus

    Build a foundation of stability and strength for the months to come

Congratulations!

You're on the first step of a long and beautiful journey. Your body is busy making that sweet little baby and you'd like to make some progress toward your goals.

You may feel tired. I mean, exhausted. But you'd like to stay fit and do something healthy for your body - for yourself!

What's the Program?

First trimester is the perfect time to lay a foundation of strength. Your body has just started to undergo magnificent changes, and you can keep it healthy with a tailored fitness program.

This is a 12-week program that blends cardio and strength training for a full body bootcamp. Commit to five days a week of moderate exercise through guided workouts plus "free days" that fit into YOUR schedule.

As with all our programs, First Trimester Focus comes with regular check-ins from our trainers. We get that pregnancy is hard. Let this be something that builds you up.
