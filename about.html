How We Started

We're a group of women with a common purpose: get a workout in with the kids! What started as spontaneous group workouts turned into regular meetups in gyms, at parks, with strollers.

Through trial and error, we found which workouts worked best. Short and efficient? Check. Pregnant-friendly? Check. Safe postpartum? Check. Oh, and breaks between sets to check on the kids? Double check!

Who We Are

Based in Cincinnati, OH, we're dedicated to building a fitness community that supports all moms. We meet up for in person workouts and stay in touch through virtual challenges.

We push each other to get fit and stay healthy. Come join us and see what we're all about!

About Peapod Fitness

Our mission is to help moms look and feel their best while pregnant and beyond. We believe a healthy mom is a happy mom... and happy moms make happy kids!
